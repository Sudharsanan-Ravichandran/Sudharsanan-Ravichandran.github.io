Passionate Data Analyst

Hi, I'm Sudharsanan

Have you ever wondered how a single dashboard can transform decision-making for an entire organization? That's the question that drives my passion for data analytics. I'm Sudharsanan Ravichandran, a data enthusiast who loves turning complex datasets into simple, actionable insights.

I've always been fascinated by the power of data to tell stories and solve problems. My journey into data analytics began with a simple realization: behind every number is an opportunity to create meaningful change. When I started my career, I was overwhelmed by the complexity of large datasets and the challenge of making sense of them. Through years of learning SQL, Power BI, and Python—and countless late nights troubleshooting errors—I've transformed from a curious beginner into a confident data analyst who creates dashboards that empower businesses to thrive.

The turning point in my career came during my Master Thesis project at Magna International, where I developed a multilingual Power BI dashboard for real-time KPI tracking. Seeing how my work helped streamline operations across global teams was the moment I realized the true impact of data analytics. This experience fueled my passion for creating intuitive visualizations that bring data to life.

Imagine a dashboard where vibrant visuals reveal hidden trends, heatmaps highlight key opportunities, and interactive filters put decision-making at your fingertips. That's what I strive to create every day. For instance, I once designed a Diversity & Inclusion dashboard that allowed HR leaders to track gender representation in leadership roles across departments in real time, helping them identify areas for improvement and take actionable steps toward inclusivity.

In my previous role at SL Corporation, I automated production analysis workflows using Excel VBA, reducing manual effort by 50% and saving over 200 hours annually for the team. These concrete achievements demonstrate my commitment to efficiency and innovation in data analytics.

I still remember the first time I presented a dashboard to a leadership team. Watching their excitement as they uncovered insights they hadn't seen before made me realize this is more than just work—it's about empowering people with knowledge they didn't know they had.

Currently, I'm part of the AI Business Fellowship at Perplexity, where I'm exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence and data analytics. Whether it's crafting dashboards or automating workflows, I'm here to make data work for you.

Let's connect! Whether you're looking for someone to build your next big dashboard or simply want to chat about data analytics trends, feel free to reach out—I'd love to hear from you.
